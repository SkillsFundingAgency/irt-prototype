Course description

This challenging course will develop students' knowledge and understanding of English Literature and Language through the study of novels, plays and poetry. It is a course which combines aspects of linguistic study with the analysis of literary and non-literary texts.

Entry requirements

Students should have achieved a Grade 4 or above in GCSE English Language and Grade 6 in English Literature.
